Our Story

Roy and Christine met in junior college in 2008. They were both science majors so it made sense that they would cross paths in biology, chemistry or physics, but instead they met in Spanish 1. Yes, they hola'd and buenos dias'd enough on their tests to pass and make it all the way to Spanish numero dos. At the time RateMyProfessor was the Yelp of every college campus and Roy used this to influence Christine to switch their current class to a more relaxed non-homework assigning professor. They started carpooling with each other after they found out they both lived a mere 1.7 miles from one another and developed a solid friendship. When they weren't in class they hung out or were constantly texting and emailing each other, until they both realized the friendship had changed.

In June of '09 Christine invited Roy to a friend's birthday celebration and after some liquid courage they both finally admitted they had feelings for each other. Flash forward (through birthdays, weddings, vacations, Coachella, both of them getting into a Radiology program) to the night of August 30, 2015 when Roy and Christine were hanging out with a couple friends in Palos Verdes and decided to go the hiking trails near the cliffside. After hiking away from their friends Roy stopped close to the edge of the cliff, kneeled down on one knee and proposed. After Christine accepted he lifted her in to the air which freaked her out because she could see the ocean at the bottom. Presently Roy and Christine plan on celebrating their union together with all of their loved ones together in Santa Barbara, CA on February 25th, 2017.
